Privacy & Safeguarding

How we protect your personal data, and the children and vulnerable people we work with.

Data Privacy Policy

Wasil GreenHer Foundation ("the Foundation", "we") is committed to protecting personal data in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and other applicable data protection regulations.

What we collect

Through this website we may collect the information you submit voluntarily: your name, email address, phone number, location, and the content of your messages, via our contact, volunteer, partnership, event registration and newsletter forms.

How we use it

We use your data only to respond to your enquiry, process your volunteer or partnership application, register you for events, and (where you have opted in) send you our newsletter. We do not sell or rent personal data to anyone.

Storage and sharing

Data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized team members who need it for the purposes above. We share data with third parties only where required by law or with your explicit consent.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time, and you may unsubscribe from the newsletter at any point. To exercise these rights, contact us.

Child & Vulnerable Persons Safeguarding

The safety and dignity of the children and vulnerable people we serve come before everything else we do. Our safeguarding commitments include:

  • All staff and volunteers complete safeguarding orientation and sign our Code of Conduct before working with children or vulnerable groups.
  • Photographs of children are taken and published only with informed consent from parents, guardians or schools, and never in a way that could put a child at risk.
  • We do not publish personal details (full names, addresses, schools) of children alongside their images.
  • Programme activities are designed to be safe, inclusive and accessible, including for persons with disabilities.
  • Anyone can report a safeguarding concern confidentially via our contact form (select "Safeguarding concern"). All reports are treated seriously and handled with confidentiality.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may log standard technical information (such as IP address) when serving font files.
