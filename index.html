Who We Are

A youth-led foundation for people and planet

Wasil GreenHer Foundation was founded in 2025 by Wasilat Kasim Imam, a Public Health professional and youth advocate, to address the interconnected challenges of gender inequality, limited educational opportunity, public health gaps, environmental degradation and youth unemployment.

  • Community-led programmes designed with the people they serve
  • Evidence-based interventions in education, health and climate
  • A special focus on girls, children, persons with disabilities and vulnerable communities
